Frequently Asked Questions

Can products be shipped internationally?

Yes! Please fill in the checkout form correctly with your shipping details and we will send it to you within 2 weeks.

Do tutors teach internationally?

All of our tutors are based in the United Kingdom so although we tutor students internationally, please note that all lessons will be held using the GMT (from October to March) and BST (from March to October) time zones. All times will be confirmed during your consultation as well as in an email after confirmation of payment for the lessons.

I bought the wrong product, what should I do?

No problem, we understand that this can happen. Please contact us as soon as possible at info@studyhaven.com. Please ensure that the product is still in its new condition.

How do I refund a product?

We are sorry that our product did not suit your needs. Please contact us within 7 days of purchase at info@studyhaven.com.

Do you have discount codes?

Yes but our discount codes are only offered to registered users who have opted in to our mailing list. Register an account here!

How long will it take to receive my order after purchase?

Please allow 3-5 days for UK shipping. For International shipping, please allow up to 2 weeks. If you still haven't received your order past these durations, please contact us at info@studyhaven.com
